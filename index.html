Who we are

We are TLD Designs, a design team based in Bristol. We love creating beautiful web pages and apps.

What we believe

The web should be for all - Elegant, beautiful and secure.

It shouldn’t be expensive to get your business, charity, group or product its space on the web. That means a website, yes, but also email and apps. It should be easy for you to tell your customers, members or anyone who’s interested who you are and what you do. Anyone who wants to read you site should be able to from whatever device they choose, desktop or mobile. From telling the world about your products and services to blogging about your fundraising activities, you should be able to have the site you want for your needs.

That’s where we come in.

What we do

We’ll build you the site you want at a reasonable cost. If you just want a website, that’s fine we can do that for you. If you need secure web hosting, we do that too and will manage and support everything. If you need the whole package: website, secure hosting, domain names and email all in one place for one price well, that’s what we love doing!

Why we do this

Quite simply, it’s fun!

We’re lucky to be the kind of people who are interested in things like HTML5 and JavaScript. We like having a good reason for researching new and cutting-edge web technologies, have a look at some of our apps in development if you’d like to know more. But we know that not everyone is like this. If you’re a plumber, a builder or pop-up coffee shop you don’t want to have to worry about cross-browser support issues or configuring SSL/TLS on IIS. If you’re trying to get your new magazine off the ground, you just don’t care about the differences between an ‘A’ record and a CNAME in DNS – You just want a site that tells people about your business or cause with no fuss, no complications and at a price that won’t break the bank.

So that’s why we do it - we enjoy it, we believe in it and we don’t think anyone else is doing it.

What we offer

If you’re an individual, sole trader, start-up or small business we’ll get you up and running on the web easily and quickly. Just tell us what you want your site to say or do and we’ll do the rest: design, build and write your copy, register your domain name and host it all securely. If you want us to arrange your email, we’ll do that too. All for a reasonable setup price and a modest support cost.

If you’re a charity, not-for-profit or similar, we’ll even do the design and build for free. If you want something a little bit different, like web or mobile apps, we do that too.
